                        🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Issue: Import errors
Fix:   source .venv/bin/activate

Issue: CUDA not found
Fix:   Don't worry! MPS (Apple Silicon) is being used automatically

Issue: Out of memory
Fix:   Reduce batch_size in configs/model_config.yaml to 16 or 8

Issue: Slow training
Fix:   1. Verify MPS is active: python -c "import torch; print(torch.backends.mps.is_available())"
       2. Reduce model complexity in configs/model_config.yaml

Issue: Dataset download fails
Fix:   Check internet connection and retry: make download
